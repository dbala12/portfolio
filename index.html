Hello! I’m Daranee. I am a journalist graduating from the University of Maryland’s Philip Merrill College of Journalism in May 2025. I was born and raised in Malaysia in an Indian and Sri Lankan family and came to the U.S. in 2023 to pursue journalism.

Through my experiences back home and in Maryland, I found how powerful investigative journalism is and am learning how to unearth and tell stories that impact people of color and other marginalized communities using data.

At Merrill College, I worked as a reporter on a couple of projects for the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism, including a project that looked into how tech organizations use travel to lobby policymakers. I was also a reporter for the Data and Washington D.C. Bureau at Merrill’s Capital News Service where I reported on the 2024 election, policies affecting the local environment, students and federal workers.
